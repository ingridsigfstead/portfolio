Photo Studio Intern

Marketing Intern

Using Klayvio, I designed playful and compelling email marketing campaigns, sending them out bi-weekly to our customers and subscribers. Pottery Barn - Williams Sonoma Inc.

Merchandising Intern

Over the summer of 2023, I served as a merchandising intern at Pottery Barn, focusing on their home and decorative accessories division. Working closely with my manager, who oversaw purchases for wall hangings, mirrors, and frames, I gained insights into analyzing past customer buying patterns to optimize inventory for future sales. I also learned the intricacies of strategically purchasing inventory for upcoming seasons. Utilizing various Excel documents, engaging in collaborations, and conducting thorough research, I contributed to the selection process for Pottery Barn's Summer 2024 merchandise. Additionally, the internship afforded us the opportunity to identify and explore a "white space" within our departments, culminating in presentations of our findings to Williams Sonoma Inc. executives.
